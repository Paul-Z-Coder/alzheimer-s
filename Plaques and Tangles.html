Plaques and Tangles:

  1. Plaques:
    • Plaques are deposits of a protein fragment named beta-amyloid
    • Plaques are found in the area between nerves
  2. Tangles:
    • Tangles are fibers of a protein named tau
    • Tangles buld up inside the cells
  3. Though scientists don't know for sure, many experts beleave that Plaques and Tangles block communication between cells.
