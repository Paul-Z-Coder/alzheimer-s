Stages Of Alzheimer’s




The stages of Alzheimer’s are:

  1. Mild/Early Alzheimer’s:
    • Cognitive difficulties incuding Memory loss
    • Problems handling money
    • Taking a long time to do normal everyday tasks
    • Changing Behavior and Personatity
  2. Moderate Alzheimer’s:
    • Reasoning,Thought, and Language trouble
    • Sensory prossesing damage, difficuly prossesing sounds and smells
    • Not being able to recognise friends and family
    • Hallucinations, Paranoia
    • Act impulsively
  3. Severe/Late Alzheimer’s
    • Brain tissue gets smaller
    • Cannot talk
    • Completley depends on others
    • At the end they might spend all the time in bed
